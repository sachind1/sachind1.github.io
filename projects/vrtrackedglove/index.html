Virtual Reality

Tracked Glove

A glove is fitted with a 9-axis inertial measurement unit and 5 flexure sensors. A Microsoft Xbox Kinect is used to track the movement of the glove in three-dimensional space. The sensor data is incorporated into Unity, with the eventual goal of integrating with augmented and virtual reality headsets.

Objectives

Create a device that tracks the movement and rotation of the user's hand without impeding it in the way that current VR controllers do.

Because our glove is securely attached to the hand the user does not need to physically hold the device, and is free to move their hand in a variety of natural forms. We take advantage of this by including flex sensors on each of the five fingers. This gives the programmer more control options when developing software for the glove.
